I
Invalid_conversion [SIunits.Value]
The exception that is raised by SIunits.Value.scale_to and SIunits.Value.convert when a value cannot be converted into the given units.
Invalid_units [SIunits.Value]
The exception that is raised by SIunits.Value.(+:), SIunits.Value.(-:) and SIunits.Value.(<-:) when two values cannot be combined due to incompatible units.
